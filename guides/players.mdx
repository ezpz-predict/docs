## What you can do

As a player on ezpz.fi you can:

- Browse and trade on sports, crypto, and pump.fun markets
- Build soccer parlays
- Track positions and P&amp;L in your portfolio
- Withdraw USDC to an external wallet

## Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect wallet">
    Click **Connect** in the header and approve with Phantom or Solflare.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optional — bind email">
    Go to **Settings** (`/settings`) to add email and enable MFA — see [Account & security](/guides/account-security).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fund your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open wallet">
    Click your balance in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    Use the deposit flow to add USDC. On devnet, a faucet may be available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Place a single prediction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a market">
    Browse the home feed, **Sports** (`/sports`), **Crypto** (`/crypto`), or **Pump.fun** (`/pumpfun`). For pump.fun coin events, see [Trade pump.fun markets](/trading/pumpfun-predictions).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the trade panel">
    Click a market card or open an event page (`/event/[slug]`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your side">
    Choose YES, NO, or the outcome you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter stake and confirm">
    Review the fee breakdown and potential payout, then click **Confirm**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

See [Place a prediction](/trading/place-prediction) for a detailed walkthrough.

## Build a parlay

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add legs">
    From soccer markets, tap **+** to add selections to your parlay slip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the slip">
    Open **Parlay** (`/parlay`) to see combined odds, fees, and max payout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place parlay">
    Enter your stake and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  All legs must be from different matches. You cannot parlay two markets on the same fixture.
</Warning>

## Track your predictions

Open [Portfolio](/guides/portfolio) (`/portfolio`) to see:

- Open positions with current value
- Settled predictions and P&amp;L history
- Open orders (when CLOB is available)

## Track your predictions

See [Deposit & withdraw](/trading/deposit-withdraw) for the full flow.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open wallet">
    Click your balance in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw">
    Enter the destination address and amount, then confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  MFA may be required for withdrawals. Enable it in **Settings** — see [Account & security](/guides/account-security).
</Note>

## Time cutoffs

| Rule | When it applies |
|------|-----------------|
| Prediction cutoff | 15 minutes before event end — placing new predictions is blocked |
| Resolution cooldown | 2 hours after expiry — operator resolves |
| Dispute window | 24 hours after resolution — redemption blocked |

## After resolution

| Outcome | Action |
|---------|--------|
| You win | [Redeem](/trading/redeem) tokens after dispute window |
| Market voided | [Refund](/trading/refund) stake |
| Wrong resolution | [File a dispute](/concepts/resolution#disputes) within 24h |

## Restricted regions

If you are in a restricted jurisdiction, you are redirected to [ezpz.fi/restricted](https://ezpz.fi/restricted) and cannot trade. See [Geographic restrictions](/resources/geographic-restrictions).
