## What you can do

As a player on ezpz.fi you can:

- Browse and trade on sports, crypto, and pump.fun markets
- Build soccer parlays
- Track positions and P&amp;L in your portfolio
- Surrender (cash out) eligible bets before events end
- Withdraw USDC to an external wallet

## Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect wallet">
    Click **Connect** in the header and approve with Phantom or Solflare.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optional — bind email">
    Go to **Settings** (`/settings`) to add email and enable MFA for account security.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fund your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open wallet">
    Click your balance in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    Use the deposit flow to add USDC. On devnet, a faucet may be available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Place a single bet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a market">
    Browse the home feed, **Sports** (`/sports`), **Crypto** (`/crypto`), or **Pump.fun** (`/pumpfun`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the trade panel">
    Click a market card or open an event page (`/event/[slug]`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your side">
    Choose YES, NO, or the outcome you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter stake and confirm">
    Review the fee breakdown and potential payout, then click **Confirm**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

See [Place a bet](/trading/place-bet) for a detailed walkthrough.

## Build a parlay

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add legs">
    From soccer markets, tap **+** to add selections to your parlay slip.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the slip">
    Open **Parlay** (`/parlay`) to see combined odds, fees, and max payout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place parlay">
    Enter your stake and confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  All legs must be from different matches. You cannot parlay two markets on the same fixture.
</Warning>

## Track your bets

Open **Portfolio** (`/portfolio`) to see:

- Open positions with current value
- Settled bets and P&amp;L history
- Open orders (when CLOB is available)

## Surrender (cash out)

You can exit some bets early if:

- The event is more than **20 minutes** away
- The market allows surrender (maker-configured deduction applies)

Default surrender split on a $100 bet with 50% deduction:

| Recipient | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| You | $50 |
| Maker | $45 |
| Platform | $5 |

For parlays, **every leg** must be 20+ minutes from its event end. If any leg is too close, surrender is blocked.

## Withdraw

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open wallet">
    Click your balance in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw">
    Enter the destination address and amount, then confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  MFA may be required for withdrawals. Enable it in **Settings**.
</Note>

## Time cutoffs

| Rule | When it applies |
|------|-----------------|
| Bet cutoff | 15 minutes before event end — placing new bets is blocked |
| Surrender cutoff | 20 minutes before event end — cash-out disabled |
| Resolution cooldown | 2 hours after expiry — operator resolves |
| Dispute window | 24 hours after resolution — redemption blocked |

## Restricted regions

If you are in a restricted jurisdiction, you are redirected to `/restricted` and cannot trade. Geographic restrictions are enforced at the platform level.
