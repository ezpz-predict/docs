**Portfolio** (`/portfolio`) is your hub for open predictions, settled history, and post-resolution actions.

## What you see

| Section | Contents |
|---------|----------|
| **Open positions** | Single-market tokens and active parlays |
| **Settled** | Resolved predictions with realized P&amp;L |
| **Actions** | Redeem, refund, sell when available |

Each row shows the market, side, stake, current value, and status.

## Single-market positions

Open single-market positions show:

- **Side** — YES or NO tokens held
- **Entry price** — average cost per token
- **Current value** — implied value from AMM price
- **Unrealized P&amp;L** — mark-to-market gain or loss
- **Status** — Open, Closed, Resolved, Voided

### Actions on open positions

| Action | When available |
|--------|----------------|
| [Sell](/trading/sell-prediction) | Market open; pool has liquidity |
| View market | Always |

## After resolution

| Market status | Your action |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Resolved** (dispute window active) | Wait — redemption blocked |
| **Resolved** (dispute window passed) | [Redeem](/trading/redeem) winning tokens |
| **Voided** | [Refund](/trading/refund) stake |
| **Disputed** | Wait for operator decision |

You can [file a dispute](/concepts/resolution#disputes) within 24 hours of resolution if you believe the outcome is wrong.

## Parlays

Parlay rows show **per-leg status** (pending, won, lost, void). The parlay settles when all legs reach a final state:

- All win → max payout
- Any loss → $0
- All void → full refund

See [Build a parlay](/trading/build-parlay).

## Activity history

Portfolio and **Activity** show transaction history: mints, swaps, redemptions, refunds, and withdrawals. Failed trades appear as reverted — your balance is not debited unless the transaction confirmed.

## CLOB orders (future)

When order-book trading launches, Portfolio will also show **open limit orders** and fill history. M1 is AMM-only for retail.
