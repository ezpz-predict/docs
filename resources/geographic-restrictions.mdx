Prediction markets are regulated differently around the world. ezpz.fi applies **geographic restrictions** to comply with applicable laws. Restrictions may vary by product vertical.

## What you may see

| Experience | Meaning |
|------------|---------|
| **Full access** | Browse and trade all available products |
| **Browse-only** | View markets; some or all trading CTAs disabled |
| **Redirect to restricted page** | Trading unavailable in your jurisdiction |

If you are redirected to [ezpz.fi/restricted](https://ezpz.fi/restricted), trading is not available in your region. Browse-only access may still work for some content.

## Product-level gating

Restrictions are not always all-or-nothing. ezpz.fi may gate specific verticals:

| Product | Typical gating |
|---------|----------------|
| **Sports** | Often restricted in regulated jurisdictions |
| **Crypto (Limit/Market)** | May remain available when sports is restricted |
| **Crypto (1-Tap / Up-Down)** | May be restricted separately (high-velocity products) |
| **Pump.fun** | Follows maker-market rules + region policy |
| **Parlays** | Follows sports restrictions (soccer legs) |

When restricted, trade buttons show as **disabled** with a tooltip explaining the policy. A **region indicator** may appear in the header.

## Before you trade

- Confirm you are legally permitted to use prediction markets in your jurisdiction
- Check whether your region allows the specific product you want to trade
- Do not attempt to circumvent geoblocking with VPNs if prohibited by terms of service

<Warning>
  Geographic policies change as regulations evolve. The in-app `/restricted` page and disabled CTAs are authoritative — do not rely on outdated documentation for eligibility.
</Warning>

## Why restrictions exist

Geographic gating supports compliance with:

- Local gambling and prediction-market laws
- Sanctions and export controls
- Anti-money laundering (AML) requirements
- Platform licensing obligations
