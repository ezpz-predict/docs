**Surrender** lets you cash out an open prediction before the event concludes — if time and market rules allow. You receive a partial refund; the maker and platform keep a configured share.

## Eligibility

You can surrender when **all** of these are true:

- The event is more than **20 minutes** away
- The market allows surrender (maker-configured)
- The market is still **Open** (before prediction cutoff)

For **parlays**, every leg must be 20+ minutes from its event end. If any leg is too close, surrender is blocked for the entire parlay.

## Default split

On a **$100 prediction** with **50% surrender deduction**:

| Recipient | Amount |
|-----------|--------|
| You | $50 |
| Maker | $45 |
| Platform | $5 |

The maker sets the surrender deduction percentage when creating the market.

## How to surrender

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Portfolio">
    Go to **Portfolio** (`/portfolio`) or open the prediction detail.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check eligibility">
    Confirm the **Surrender** button is enabled (not greyed out).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review breakdown">
    Read the refund amount, maker share, and platform fee.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Surrender** to execute the cash-out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When surrender is blocked

| Reason | Message / behavior |
|--------|------------------|
| Event within 20 minutes | Surrender cutoff — use AMM sell if pool has liquidity |
| Prediction cutoff passed | Market closed — wait for resolution or void |
| Parlay leg too close | Entire parlay surrender blocked |
| Restricted product | Region gating may disable trading CTAs |

## Surrender vs selling

| Method | When to use |
|--------|-------------|
| **Surrender** | Platform-defined early exit with fixed deduction |
| **AMM sell** | Sell tokens into the pool at current market price (if liquidity exists) |

Selling via the AMM may give a better or worse price depending on pool depth and odds movement.
