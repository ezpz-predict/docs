## Find pump.fun markets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the feed">
    Go to **Pump.fun** (`/pumpfun`) from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse or search">
    The grid lists **platform-created markets only** — coins with at least one published question on ezpz. Use the search box to filter by title.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a coin event">
    Click a card to open the coin event page (`/pumpfun/[slug]`) with price chart, market list, and trade panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If the feed is empty, no pump.fun markets have been published yet. Makers and community authors create them via [Author coin](/guides/pumpfun-creators).
</Note>

## Read the coin event page

| Element | What it tells you |
|---------|-------------------|
| **Coin header** | Name, ticker, live price, 24h change (from pump.fun data) |
| **Verified creator chip** | This market's author linked the coin's pump.fun creator wallet |
| **Market rows** | Each synthetic question with Yes/No prices and expiry |
| **Price chart** | Recent coin price history |
| **Trade panel** | Stake, fees, shares, and potential payout for the selected market |

A single coin can have multiple markets — graduation, different mcap thresholds, and rolling five-minute up/down windows.

## Market shapes and what to expect

### Graduation and market-cap threshold

These markets are **open for trading** as soon as they are published and seeded. Read the title and resolution rules before confirming:

- **Graduation:** YES wins if the coin graduates from pump.fun by expiry.
- **Mcap threshold:** YES wins if USD market cap at expiry is at or above the stated target.

### 5-minute up / down

These markets move through two phases:

| Phase | UI state | Can you trade? |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| **Pending** | "Opens at HH:MM UTC" — reference price not yet set | No |
| **Open** | Price to Beat shown — five-minute window live | Yes |
| **Closed / resolved** | Outcome declared | Redeem or move on |

The **Price to Beat** is the reference captured at window open. YES (Up) wins if the coin's price at window close is **≥** that reference.

<Warning>
  Up/down windows are short. Predictions still follow the global **15-minute cutoff** rule relative to event end where applicable — for a five-minute window, trading closes when the window ends.
</Warning>

## Place a prediction

The trade flow matches other verticals. See [Place a prediction](/trading/place-prediction) for the full panel breakdown.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a market">
    On the coin event page, click the market row you want (or open it from the `/pumpfun` grid).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose YES or NO">
    For up/down markets, YES = Up and NO = Down.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter stake and review">
    Check price, fees, shares, and potential payout. Large stakes move the AMM price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click **Confirm**. The platform executes mint + swap on your custodial wallet — no per-trade wallet popup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After you predict

| Scenario | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Win** | [Redeem](/trading/redeem) after resolution and the dispute window |
| **Lose** | Tokens expire worthless |
| **Sell early** | [Sell](/trading/sell-prediction) via AMM if the pool has liquidity |
| **Voided market** | [Refund](/trading/refund) your stake |

Track everything in **Portfolio** (`/portfolio`).

## Parlays

Pump.fun markets **cannot** be added to parlays yet. Parlays are soccer-only today. Build single predictions on pump.fun instead.

## Resolution timing

Most pump.fun shapes resolve automatically via platform crons shortly after expiry:

| Shape | Typical resolution |
|-------|-------------------|
| Graduation | Graduation flag at expiry |
| Mcap threshold | Recorded market cap at expiry |
| Up/down | Price comparison at window close |

After on-chain resolution, the standard **24-hour dispute window** applies before redemption. See [Resolution](/concepts/resolution).

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Likely cause | What to do |
|-------|--------------|------------|
| Market not yet open | Up/down still pending | Wait for reference capture at window open |
| Empty `/pumpfun` feed | No published markets | Check back later or ask makers in the community |
| Trade disabled near expiry | Cutoff passed | Market closed to new predictions |
| Non-soccer parlay error | Pump.fun leg in parlay slip | Remove the leg; parlays are soccer-only |

More fixes: [Troubleshooting](/resources/troubleshooting).
