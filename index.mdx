## What is ezpz.fi?

ezpz.fi is a prediction market where you trade on real-world outcomes (i.e.) sports matches, crypto price moves, pump.fun coins, tokenized assets price movements, geopolitics and more. Prices reflect collective belief about what will happen. Markets settle on **Solana** with **USDC** collateral for now. We will be allowing more stablecoins in the near future.

You sign in once; the platform handles on-chain execution through a **self-custodial wallet** so you can make predictions without approving every transaction. Ensuring lightning fast reaction time for all traders, makers and jeeters alike.

## How it works

| Step | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| **1. Deposit** | Fund your custodial wallet with USDC |
| **2. Predict** | Buy YES or NO on a market via the AMM |
| **3. Hold or exit** | Wait for resolution or sell via the AMM |
| **4. Redeem** | Winning tokens pay \$1.00 USDC each |

## Who this documentation is for

- **Players** — place single predictions and parlays, track positions, withdraw funds
- **Makers** — create and publish markets, configure odds, earn maker fees
- **Operators** — resolve markets, handle disputes, manage treasury (oracle-admin console)

<Note>
  ezpz.fi does not offer a public API for third-party integrations. All trading happens through the web app.
</Note>
